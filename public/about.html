About Me

I am a motivated and passionate individual on a continuous journey to develop and excel in my career. My pursuit began right after completing my matriculation when I embarked on a digital marketing course. This experience not only honed my understanding of marketing techniques but also led me to work as a brand consultant at ORIFLAME, a renowned Swedish cosmetic company, where I contributed for nearly 12 to 13 months.

During my intermediate years, I embraced the world of graphic designing, a field that I find endlessly fascinating and creative. While I may not consider myself an expert, I have successfully designed logos and various formats for a real estate company, among other small-scale projects. It's also worth noting that I achieved the Microsoft Office Specialist certification during this time.

My skills in graphic designing and digital marketing proved invaluable when I ventured into entrepreneurship with my own small book business, which thrived for a year before I decided to pursue even greater endeavors.

Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Data Sciences at FAST NUCES, one of Pakistan's esteemed universities. In addition to my academic pursuits, I am an active member of two remarkable societies: the Google Developer Students Club and FAST Business Club, where I contribute as a graphic designer.

My journey is far from over; I am dedicated to continuously enhancing my skills and aspiring to become an expert in my chosen fields.
